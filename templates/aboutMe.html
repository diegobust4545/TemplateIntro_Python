A little intro about me.

I have many hobbies that I find fulfilment in my life from collecting records to doing photography. My love for photography arose when I took a Photo Journalism class when I was a sophmore in highschool. There I learned how to use a SLR. I learned everything from how to shoot manual, rules of compositions to how to develop film in a dark room. Now I currently work for Salem Fields Community Church as a Production assisant, and part of the job requires me to be their photographer for large events. Aside from that, I still love to shoot for my own enjoyment and will continue to pursue this art.
